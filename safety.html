SAFETY?

WELCOME TO THE SAFEST SWING ON THE PLANET

Let’s face it—swinging off a cliff isn’t exactly something you do every day. But don’t worry! At ManaliSwing , we’ve turned safety into an art form (and maybe even a science). Here’s why taking the leap with us is as safe as binge-watching Netflix in bed.

Our Jump Masters aren’t just regular adrenaline junkies—they’re engineers, artists, rock climbers, mountaineers, and basically superheroes in human form. They undergo years of rigorous training in rope access, mid-air rescues, advanced first aid, and more. But let’s face it—even Superman has an off day. That’s where our AI steps in as the ultimate safety sidekick. If a Jump Master somehow misses even the tiniest detail after their 10,000+ hours of training (unlikely, but hey, nobody’s perfect), the AI swoops in like an overprotective mom, slamming the brakes faster than your mom yelling “Stop!” during a horror movie!

So rest assured, whether it’s human expertise or tech wizardry, we’ve got you covered—literally!

1. AI That Watches Your Back (Literally)

Our AI-powered safety system is like the Hermione Granger of adventure sports—it knows everything and never misses a detail. Developed by our tech wizard Siddharth and his team from IIT Hyderabad & IIT Mandi, this AI has been trained on 3 million+ images to ensure 97%+ accuracy in spotting potential risks before they happen. Whether it’s checking ropes, tracking jump protocols, or making sure no one sneaks onto the platform wearing flip-flops, our AI has got your back—literally!

2. Tested, Trusted & Approved by Physics Itself

Before anyone jumped off our swing, we put it through more tests than a college entrance exam. First came 1,000 dummy jumps —yes, dummies love adventure too! Then, under the watchful eyes of IRATA & International experts (the rock stars of rope access), we completed successful human trials at IIT Hyderabad. Spoiler alert: Everyone landed safely—and some even asked if they could go again immediately.

Fun Fact: Our retrieval system is so innovative that it brings clients back to the platform instead of dropping them on the ground. Why? Because we care about your knees almost as much as you do. Also, This makes rescues 3x safer and allows us to serve 2x more thrill-seekers per day . Translation? Less waiting, more swinging!

3. World-Class Gear You Can Trust

When it comes to equipment, we didn’t just settle for “good enough.” Nope, we partnered with global leaders like Hilti India , Dillon USA , and Karam International —brands trusted by NASA, SpaceX, and other cool people with rockets. From aerospace-grade aluminum structures to state-of-the-art safety harnesses, everything is built to withstand forces stronger than your grandma’s hugs.

Ever heard of a superhero arriving in under 3 minutes? That’s us. Thanks to our crew’s years of training and advanced retrieval system, we can rescue anyone mid-air and bring them back to the platform quicker than you can say “Oops!” And with multiple layers of redundancy, our systems are almost fail-proof. Seriously, even our backups have backups. It’s like carrying an umbrella inside another umbrella—just in case.

5. Redefining Adventure—Because Ordinary Is Overrated

Did we mention we’re pioneers? While most giant swings rely on old-school tech imported from the west,, ManaliSwing built its own cutting-edge proprietary technology from scratch.
